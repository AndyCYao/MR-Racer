BRIDGE ENGINE EXAMPLES
======================

There are three examples in "Scenes" folder:
VR Example.unity - Demonstrates full VR immersion with a Collision Avoidance shader
MR Example.unity - Demonstrates Mixed Reality, and interaction using GVR pointing system.
MR Example Without GoogleVR.unity - Demonstrates Mixed Reality without GoogleVR dependencies, but no pointing system.

• Build your iOS Project
• Connect Structure Sensor
• Connect Bridge Controller
• Scan a scene for tracking on
• Enjoy!
